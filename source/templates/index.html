    About Us

    Health Cognizant is a web-based application that uses machine learning to predict the likelihood of various diseases based on user-inputted data. Our goal is to provide users with a simple and accessible tool to assess their health risks and take proactive steps towards a healthier lifestyle.

    A new way to keep track of your health, with the power of AI

    Our platform provides a suite of tools to help you monitor your health and predict potential risks for various diseases. We leverage the power of machine learning to provide you with accurate and timely information.

    • Get predictions for 5 different diseases.
    • Answer a simple questionnaire to get recommended tests.
    • Get diet and lifestyle recommendations based on your results.

    Our goal is to empower you with the information you need to make informed decisions about your health. We believe that preventative care is the key to a long and healthy life.

    Cardiology

    The cardiology section of our application helps you to predict the risk of heart disease based on various factors such as age, sex, and cholesterol levels.

    We use a machine learning model trained on a large dataset of heart disease patients to provide you with an accurate prediction. Our model takes into account various factors such as chest pain type, resting blood pressure, and maximum heart rate achieved to provide you with a comprehensive risk assessment.

    Neurology

    The neurology section of our application helps you to predict the risk of Parkinson's disease based on various vocal features.

    Our model uses a variety of vocal measurements, such as fundamental frequency, jitter, and shimmer, to predict the likelihood of Parkinson's disease. This can help in early detection and management of the disease.

    Hepatology

    The hepatology section of our application helps you to predict the risk of liver disease based on various factors such as age, gender, and blood test results.

    Our model uses a variety of blood test results, such as albumin, alkaline phosphatase, and total bilirubin, to predict the likelihood of liver disease. This can help in early detection and management of the disease.

    Pediatrics

    The pediatrics section of our application helps you to predict the risk of pneumonia in children based on chest X-ray images.

    Our model uses a deep learning model trained on a large dataset of pediatric chest X-ray images to provide an accurate prediction of pneumonia. This can help in early detection and treatment of the disease in children.
